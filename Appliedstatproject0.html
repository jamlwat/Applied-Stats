We will now perform a bootstrap mean on RBIs and compare this to our mean we computed early on in Module 0. Original Hypothesis test: Null Hypothesis- True mean is equivalent to 15 RBIs Alternative Hypothesis- True mean is not equivalent to 15 RBIs

library(boot)

boot_mean <- function(df, indices) {
  return(mean(df[indices]))
